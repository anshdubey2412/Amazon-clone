With a history of more than two and a half centuries, the Defence Accounts Department (DAD) is one of the oldest departments under the Government of India. Headed by Controller General of Defence Accounts (CGDA), the department is responsible for Financial Advice, Payment, Accounting and Internal Audit in respect of Expenditure and Receipts of the Defence Services and other Defence related organisations such as DRDO, Ordnance Factories, Coast Guard and Border Roads..
